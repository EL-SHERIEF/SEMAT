Effective Date: 2080/8/08
Last Updated: 2025/2/23

Introduction

Welcome to Semat. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with our AI-driven technologies.

Information We Collect

We collect different types of information to improve our services and enhance user experience, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details when you sign up or communicate with us.

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and usage data collected automatically when you use our platform.

  • AI Interaction Data: Data processed through our AI technologies, which may include text, images, and other forms of input provided by users.

  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to analyze trends, administer the site, and enhance user experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for:

  • Providing and improving our AI and tech-driven services.

  • Personalizing user experiences and delivering relevant content.

  • Ensuring security, detecting fraud, and preventing abuse.

  • Communicating with users about updates, offers, and support.

  • Conducting research and analytics to enhance our products.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist us in operating our business, such as hosting and analytics providers.

  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect our rights and users' safety.

  • Business Transfers: In case of mergers, acquisitions, or business restructuring, your information may be transferred.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.

  • Opt out of marketing communications.

  • Restrict or object to certain data processing activities.

  • Request data portability.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices, and we encourage you to read their policies.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically.

Terms and Conditions

By accessing and using our services, you agree to comply with and be bound by these Terms and Conditions.

2. Use of Services

You agree to use our services only for lawful purposes and in a manner that does not infringe on the rights of others.

3. Intellectual Property

4. Limitation of Liability

Semat is not liable for any damages arising from the use or inability to use our services.

5. Termination

We reserve the right to terminate or restrict access to our services for any violation of these terms.
